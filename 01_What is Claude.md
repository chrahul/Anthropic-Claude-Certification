## CONCEPT

### What Makes Claude Different

Claude is not a simple chatbot — it is an **AI assistant** designed to be your
**thinking partner**. While a basic chatbot handles straightforward
question-and-answer exchanges, Claude can tackle complex, multi-step work
across writing, research, coding, reasoning, and learning.

### Guiding Principles

Claude is built on three core principles and a training approach called
**Constitutional AI**:

| Principle    | What It Means                                                                 |
|--------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Helpful**  | Actively assists with a wide variety of conversational and text-processing tasks |
| **Harmless** | Trained to avoid toxic, discriminatory, illegal, or unethical outputs          |
| **Honest**   | Designed to operate transparently and align with human values                  |

### Core Capabilities

| Capability                        | Description                                                                                                                                                   |
|-----------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Writing & content creation**    | Collaborate on social media posts, emails, reports. Claude takes direction on personality and tone so your voice comes through.                                |
| **Research & analysis**           | Explore research angles, compile findings, analyse data. Upload documents and Claude processes them using its **large context window** (200K+ tokens by default, up to **1M tokens** on Pro/Max/Team/Enterprise with Opus 4.7 — roughly 500+ pages). |
| **Coding assistance**             | Write, debug, and explain code across multiple languages. **Claude Opus 4.7** is described as the most powerful coding model available.                        |
| **Problem-solving & reasoning**   | Handle complex cognitive tasks, maths, and strategic analysis. **Extended thinking** lets Claude work through problems step-by-step in hybrid models (Opus 4.7 & Sonnet 4.7). |
| **Learning new things**           | Adapt to your learning style and pace. **Learning mode** guides your reasoning rather than just giving answers, helping build critical thinking skills.         |

### Steerability

Claude is designed to be **steerable and collaborative** — you can direct its
personality, tone, and behaviour. This means you get your desired output with
less effort compared to other AI tools.

### Ways to Access Claude

| Interface              | Best For                                                                                       |
|------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Claude.ai**          | Conversations, writing, research, analysis, file creation (web, desktop, and mobile apps). Primary focus of this course. |
| **Claude Code**        | Agentic coding — directly edit files, run commands, create commits from your desktop.          |
| **Claude and Slack**   | Chat with Claude inside Slack channels, threads, or DMs. Claude can search your workspace for context. |
| **Claude for Excel**   | Sidebar in Microsoft Excel — analyse, modify, create workbooks, debug formulas, navigate multi-tab files. |

> **Plan availability:** Claude apps are available on Free, Pro, Max, Team,
> and Enterprise plans. Conversations, projects, memory, and preferences
> **sync across all devices** when you are signed in.
