## CONCEPT

### What Makes Claude Different

Claude is not a simple chatbot — it is an **AI assistant** designed to be your
**thinking partner**. While a basic chatbot handles straightforward
question-and-answer exchanges, Claude can tackle complex, multi-step work
across writing, research, coding, reasoning, and learning.

### Guiding Principles

Claude is built on three core principles and a training approach called
**Constitutional AI**:

| Principle    | What It Means                                                                 |
|--------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Helpful**  | Actively assists with a wide variety of conversational and text-processing tasks |
| **Harmless** | Trained to avoid toxic, discriminatory, illegal, or unethical outputs          |
| **Honest**   | Designed to operate transparently and align with human values                  |

### Core Capabilities

| Capability                        | Description                                                                                                                                                   |
|-----------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Writing & content creation**    | Collaborate on social media posts, emails, reports. Claude takes direction on personality and tone so your voice comes through.                                |
| **Research & analysis**           | Explore research angles, compile findings, analyse data. Upload documents and Claude processes them using its **large context window** (200K+ tokens by default, up to **1M tokens** on Pro/Max/Team/Enterprise with Opus 4.7 — roughly 500+ pages). |
| **Coding assistance**             | Write, debug, and explain code across multiple languages. **Claude Opus 4.7** is described as the most powerful coding model available.                        |
| **Problem-solving & reasoning**   | Handle complex cognitive tasks, maths, and strategic analysis. **Extended thinking** lets Claude work through problems step-by-step in hybrid models (Opus 4.7 & Sonnet 4.7). |
| **Learning new things**           | Adapt to your learning style and pace. **Learning mode** guides your reasoning rather than just giving answers, helping build critical thinking skills.         |

### Steerability

Claude is designed to be **steerable and collaborative** — you can direct its
personality, tone, and behaviour. This means you get your desired output with
less effort compared to other AI tools.

### Ways to Access Claude

| Interface              | Best For                                                                                       |
|------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Claude.ai**          | Conversations, writing, research, analysis, file creation (web, desktop, and mobile apps). Primary focus of this course. |
| **Claude Code**        | Agentic coding — directly edit files, run commands, create commits from your desktop.          |
| **Claude and Slack**   | Chat with Claude inside Slack channels, threads, or DMs. Claude can search your workspace for context. |
| **Claude for Excel**   | Sidebar in Microsoft Excel — analyse, modify, create workbooks, debug formulas, navigate multi-tab files. |

> **Plan availability:** Claude apps are available on Free, Pro, Max, Team,
> and Enterprise plans. Conversations, projects, memory, and preferences
> **sync across all devices** when you are signed in.

## LAB

### Lab 1 — Meet Your Thinking Partner

**Goal:** Experience Claude as a thinking partner (not just a Q&A bot) by
asking it to help you break down a real work challenge.

**Steps:**

1. Open https://claude.ai (or the desktop/mobile app).
2. Paste the following prompt:

```text
I'm preparing a quarterly business review for my team. Help me think through
the structure — what sections should I include, what questions should each
section answer, and what data would I need?
```

3. Read Claude's response. Notice how it goes beyond a simple list and offers
   reasoning about *why* each section matters.
4. Follow up with:

```text
Now adjust the tone to be more conversational — this will be presented
informally to a small leadership group.
```

**Observe:**
- Claude adapts its tone based on your direction — this is **steerability** in
  action.
- The response is structured and collaborative, not a one-line answer.

---

### Lab 2 — Explore Claude's Capabilities

**Goal:** Test at least three of Claude's core capabilities in a single
conversation to see its range.

**Steps:**

1. Start a new conversation in Claude.ai.
2. Try each of the prompts below, one after another, in the same chat:

**Writing:**
```text
Write a professional but friendly email to a client informing them that their
project timeline has shifted by two weeks. Keep it under 150 words.
```

**Reasoning:**
```text
A team of 5 engineers can complete a migration in 12 weeks. If I add 3 more
engineers but lose 2 weeks to onboarding, will the project finish sooner?
Think through this step by step.
```

**Code:**
```text
Write a Python function that takes a list of meeting durations (in minutes)
and returns the total hours and remaining minutes.
```

3. Review the outputs for each.

**Observe:**
- Notice how Claude handles each task differently — structured email vs.
  step-by-step reasoning vs. clean code with explanation.
- All three happened in a single conversation thread, showing Claude's
  versatility.

---

### Lab 3 — Find Your Use Cases

**Goal:** Identify real tasks from your own workday where Claude could act as a
thinking partner.

**Steps:**

1. Open a new conversation in Claude.ai.
2. Paste the following prompt (customise the role to match yours):

```text
I work as a Director of Cloud Engineering. Look at common tasks someone in
this role handles weekly — status reports, architecture reviews, stakeholder
emails, JIRA updates, vendor evaluations. For each task, suggest how you
could help me as a thinking partner, not just an answer machine.
```

3. Review the suggestions and pick 2–3 that match tasks on your calendar this
   week.

**Tips:**
- This lab mirrors the **Lesson Reflection** activity: map Claude's
  capabilities to your actual schedule.
- Save the conversation — you can return to it as a quick-reference for
  use-case ideas throughout the course.

## Key Takeaways

- Claude is guided by three principles — **helpful, harmless, and honest** —
  enforced through a training approach called **Constitutional AI**.
- Claude goes beyond chatbot Q&A: it handles writing, research, coding,
  reasoning, and learning as a **thinking partner**.
- **Steerability** means you can direct Claude's tone, personality, and
  behaviour to match your needs.
- Claude's **large context window** (200K–1M tokens) lets it process hundreds
  of pages in a single conversation.
- You can access the same Claude intelligence via **Claude.ai, Claude Code,
  Slack, and Excel** — synced across web, desktop, and mobile.
